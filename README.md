**The 8 Statistical "Filters"**:
1. **Binomial** - Success/failure events (seed germination)
2. **Poisson** - Rare events (species colonization)
3. **Normal** - Natural variation (plant heights)
4. **Uniform** - Equal probability (random transects)
5. **Exponential** - Waiting times (time between births)
6. **Geometric** - Trials to success (seeds until germination)
7. **Negative Binomial** - Overdispersed counts (parasites per host)
8. **Log-Normal** - Multiplicative processes (population sizes)

**Unique Pedagogical Approach**:
- ğŸ©º **Stethoscope Analogy**: Statistics as a diagnostic instrument

- ğŸ® **Interactive Sliders**: Explore parameter effects dynamically
- ğŸŒ± **Ecological Context**: Every example rooted in biology

**What You'll Learn**: Choose appropriate statistical distributions, conduct hypothesis tests correctly, understand p-values properly, design sampling strategies
