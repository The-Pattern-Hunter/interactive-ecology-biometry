### The Stethoscope Analogy ğŸ©º

Statistical distributions are **diagnostic instruments**:
- Normal distribution â†’ Detects random variation (like a stethoscope)
- Poisson distribution â†’ Detects rare events (like a thermometer)
- Exponential distribution â†’ Detects constant-rate processes (like blood pressure)

**Result**: Students understand **why** formulas exist, not just **how** to calculate.
